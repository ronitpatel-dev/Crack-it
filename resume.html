A resume is your first impression in front of employers. It is a professional summary of your education, skills, experience, and achievements. It helps hiring managers quickly understand who you are and what you bring to the table.

Showcases Your Skills & Strengths – Highlights what you’re good at.

Creates a Positive First Impression – A well-designed resume grabs attention.

Acts as a Personal Marketing Tool – Sells your abilities to potential employers.

Helps in Shortlisting – Many companies use resumes to screen candidates quickly.

Organizes Your Information Clearly – Makes your journey easy to understand.

Boosts Confidence in Interviews – Gives you talking points to discuss during the interview.
