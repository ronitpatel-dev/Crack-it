Understanding the types of interview questions is crucial for preparation. It helps you stay confident, structure your answers clearly, and avoid being caught off guard. A well-prepared candidate stands out and has a better chance of getting selected.

How to Crack These Questions Smartly:

Research the company and role – Know what they expect.

Practice common questions – Especially technical and HR-related.

Use the STAR method for behavioral answers – Situation, Task, Action, Result.

Be honest but strategic – Show learning even from failures.

Stay calm and confident – Good communication often matters more than a perfect answer.

Mock interviews help – Practice with friends or mentors to improve.
